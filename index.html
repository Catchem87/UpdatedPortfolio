About Me

Ashley has come to web development through a career transition from marketing. Having spent over three years in brand management for a pharma company, she decided it was time for a change and registered herself for Triology's "Coding Bootcamp".

The career change was scary at first, as Ashley had no previous experience or knowledge of web development. The closest she had come to web development was graphic design with the Adobe Creative Suite, but she was ready for the challenge.

From starting with zero knowledge, Ashley has now learned about HTML, CSS, javaScript, and firebase. Her bootcamp journey is now about to take her into the world of node.js. Stay tuned to see where this journey takes her.
